FOUNDER

A way to combine a love of travel, a passion for sustainability, and a desire to share the beauty and diversity of the Himachal with others, Himalayan Haven Tours was established in the year 2023 by Vasu Bhasin, who was inspired by the love of travel and a desire to show the beauty of the Himalayan region with others. To top it all, he was inspired by a desire to create a travel company that puts sustainability and responsible tourism at the forefront of its mission. By creating tours that offer immersive cultural experiences, the founder hope to create a deeper appreciation and understanding of the Himalayas among travelers.

OUR MOTIVE

Connecting travelers to the majestic beauty and cultural richness of the Himalayas through safe, sustainable and personalized tours, creating unforgettable experiences and promoting responsible tourism.
